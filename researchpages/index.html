Research Interests

My research career so far has focussed on the investigation of novel materials for semiconductor device applications. This has included solar cells, lasers & LED devices. My work has often involved the synthesis and characterisation of new semiconductor materials using a variety of techniques. In addition to my device work I have automated new experimental setups and data analyses.

Experimental Skills

  • Materials Synthesis: Solution Processed Semiconductors, Nanocrystal Synthesis
  • Device Fabrication and Optimisation: LEDs, Lasers, Photovoltaics
  • Electrical Characterisation: Current-Voltage Analysis, Accelerated Aging, Encapsulation
  • Thin Film Characterisation: XRD, SEM, EDX, Optical Microscopy, STM, AFM, Profilometry
  • Automation: Labview, Arduino, Writing Drivers, Python
  • Spectroscopy: Absorption, Photoluminescence, PLQE, Fluorometry, Ultrafast Spectroscopy Analysis
